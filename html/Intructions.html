8 steps to get better

Adjust controller settings

Fortnite provides options to customize controller sensitivity and aim assist settings. Experiment with different sensitivity settings to find what works best for you. Generally, a lower sensitivity can help with precision aiming, while a higher sensitivity allows for quicker movements. Adjust the aim assist strength based on your preference.

Practice in Creative mode

Fortnite's Creative mode offers a variety of aim training maps and scenarios created by the community. Spend time in these maps to practice your aim and develop muscle memory. Look for maps specifically designed to improve aim or aim training courses that simulate real in-game scenarios.

Use the training range

Fortnite's training range allows you to practice shooting at targets with different weapons. Use this mode to familiarize yourself with the mechanics of different guns, work on your tracking, and refine your aim.

Master different aiming techniques

There are two primary aiming techniques used in Fortnite: tracking and flick shots. Tracking involves smoothly following your target's movements, while flick shots require quickly snapping your aim to hit a target. Practice both techniques to become proficient in various situations.

Utilize aim assist

Fortnite's aim assist feature helps console players by subtly tracking targets. It's important to understand how aim assist functions and learn to use it effectively. Remember, aim assist should be seen as an aid, not a crutch. Relying solely on aim assist may limit your gcolumnth as a player.

Play with purpose

Rather than mindlessly playing, focus on specific aspects of your aiming that need improvement. Set goals for each session and actively work towards them. For example, you could focus on improving shotgun accuracy or long-range sniping.

Play regularly

Consistency is key to improving your aim. Set aside dedicated time each day or week to practice your aim. Regular practice will help build muscle memory and improve your reflexes.
